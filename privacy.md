# Privacy Policy

## 1. Controller / Operator
This app is operated by a private individual based in Switzerland and is offered worldwide via the App Store. All statements below apply globally unless noted otherwise.

## 2. Principles of Data Processing
Your data stays on **your device**. The app does not create user profiles, does not use analytics, and does not send data to servers.

## 3. What Data Is Stored
The app stores the following data **locally on your device** during use:

- **Steps** (as provided by the system)
- **Learning progress & learned vocabulary**
- **App settings**

No other data is stored.

## 4. Purpose of Storage
Stored data is used solely to enable the app’s core functions (e.g., showing your progress or tailoring exercises). No data is processed for advertising or tracking.

## 5. No Data Transfer
No personal or usage data is transmitted to the developer or to third parties. The app works offline and does not communicate with external servers.

## 6. Your Control
You can stop using the app at any time. When you **delete the app**, iOS removes the app’s local data from the device.

*Note:* device backups may still contain previous local data until they are overwritten or deleted.

## 7. Contact, Updates & Disclaimer
For privacy questions, please use the **Contact** option in the app. This notice may be updated; the latest version is shown in the app.
